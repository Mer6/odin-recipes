Ginisang Sayote

is a Filipino vegetable dish also known as Sauteed Chayote. Is a simple recipe involving one of the members of the gourd family, chayote.

Ingredients

  • Sayote
  • Ground Pork
  • Achuete
  • Tomato
  • Garlic & Onion

Steps

  1. Cut the sayote lengt-wise and remove the white core with a knife.
  2. Peel off the skin with a peeler then slice the chayote in smaller bite-sized pieces.
  3. Heat the pan and fry the garlic, onion, and tomato in oil.
  4. Ensure that the ground pork is browned before adding the sayote.
  5. Do not overcook the sayote. It should be tender with a bit of crisp in it.
